Lemonade

Squeeze, Drink, Enjoy, Repeat

Lemonade is great!

Here is a simple recipe to quench you thirst! You're only 4 steps away from deliciousness! Click above to get started!

What You Need:

  • 8 lemons
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 8 cups water
  • A large bowl
  • Something to mash with (wooden spoon or a potato masher)
  • Glass measuring cup
  • Pitcher
  • Strainer
  • Ice (if you want it chill...)

Step 1:

Wash lemons with soap and warm water. Afterwards roll the lemons on a hard surface until they become soft.

Step 2:

Slice the lemons and mash them with the sugar in a large bowl using your masher until the sugar dissolves.

Step 3:

Strain the mixture into a glass measuring cup, and then measure 2 cups. If there is less than 2 cups, then return to step 2 and mash more lemons until you have 2 cups.

Step 4:

Pour the 2 cups of mixture into a pitcher and add eight (8) cups of water to it. Stir the lemonade well. Taste test it to see if it is too sweet and add water if necessary. Your lemonade is ready to serve!
